Tuition Flash Sale!


This has been a time of frustration and excitement. It is frustrating to have to part company with remembrances from my childhood. However, it is exciting in that the sales have been overwhelmingly beneficial to paying down my tuition. I want to thank anyone for returning to the site to see what is left. I also want to welcome anyone coming here for the first time to see what is left.


My collection began in the 1970's and has continued for almost 50 years in one form or another. As collections go, it is pretty diverse with a broad scope of interest. For the most part, they cover movies and TV shows I have some emotional connection with. The only exception would be "Game of Thrones," which I purchased just because they looked cool. Sadly, I have to part with them, but I feel I can make my collection even better in the future by continuing my education.

This collection of figures has been with me starting in 1978. The figures have had a special place in my life for the following reasons:

  • I have seen all of the Star Wars movies from #4 through present in the theater beginning in 1978.
  • I have never seen an episode of GoT but own the action figure.
  • Spent manyhours playing Mario Kart Double Dash with my sons.
  • Legos were my early experience in design and engineering.
  • Spiderman is one of my top 3 superheroes.
  • Only made it through season 3 of The Walking Dead.
  • Love Sci-Fi and SG1 was a fantastic movie.
